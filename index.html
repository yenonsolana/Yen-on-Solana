The yen (¥) is the official currency of Japan, the world's third-largest economy. Since its introduction in 1871, it has become one of the most traded currencies globally, particularly on the foreign exchange market (Forex). The yen is often used as a safe-haven currency during times of global economic uncertainty, thanks to the stability of Japan's economy and its strategic position in global exports.
